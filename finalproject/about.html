About PlenoNet

Our History

PlenoNet began in Manta with the goal of providing reliable internet service to homes and businesses. From a small local startup, we have grown rapidly while maintaining our commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver fast, secure, and high-quality internet connectivity while ensuring every customer has a positive experience. We embrace best practices and constantly adopt new technologies.

Our Vision

We aim to expand our network nationwide, enhance service coverage, and be recognized as a top internet service provider, leading with innovation and excellent customer care.

Our Values

  • Customer Satisfaction
  • Innovation & Technology
  • Reliability & Quality
  • Professional Ethics

Our Team & Technology

Our dedicated team ensures smooth operations and implements the latest technology to guarantee high-speed internet services. We invest in modern equipment and network infrastructure to serve you better.
