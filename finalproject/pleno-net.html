PlenoNet - Site Plan

Site Name

PlenoNet

I chose this name because it represents a reliable and fast Internet service provider, highlighting connectivity and professionalism for homes and businesses.

Suggested domain: plenonet.com

Purpose of the Site

The purpose of PlenoNet's website is to provide users with information about our Internet services, coverage zones, pricing plans, and allow them to contact us or request service through a form. The website aims to showcase PlenoNet as a trustworthy ISP and make it easy for customers to learn and sign up.

Scenarios

  • Which PlenoNet plan is suitable for a small home office?
  • How can I check if my area is covered by PlenoNet?

Color Scheme

  • Blue (#1E90FF): Main headings, buttons, links
  • Light Gray (#f0f2f5): Page background

Typography

  • Montserrat: Headings (h1, h2, h3)
  • Open Sans: Body text and paragraphs

Pages Overview

  • Home: Company info, coverage zones, why choose PlenoNet
  • Plans: List and description of available internet plans
  • Contact: Form for users to request service or ask questions
